About Us

We are a non-profit organization that surveys the forests of Ontario, Canada. We provide information on the fauna that inhabits the forests and raise awareness on the impact of human activity on these ecosystems.

Census data and information on behaviour and diet is collected by teams from various universities accross the province. Dr. Susan Carson and Dr. Ronald James Schmidt from the University of Algoma spearheaded the initiative to properly measure the impact of human activity over time in hopes to motivate real change.
